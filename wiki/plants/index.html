Grow a Garden 2 Plant Tier List: Overview

Plants are the core of Grow a Garden 2 – you plant seeds, grow crops, and sell them for sheckles. Higher-tier plants have exponentially higher sell values, but they also cost more and may have longer growth times. Some plants have unique abilities like bouncing, low gravity, or defense. We've ranked them based on overall profitability and utility.

Tier Plants

S-Tier plants offer the highest returns and powerful abilities, while lower tiers are best for early-game farming or specific niche uses.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best plant in Grow a Garden 2?

The best plants in Grow a Garden 2 are Dragon's Breath, Moon Bloom, Venus Fly Trap, Pomegranate, and Poison Apple. These S-Tier plants have the highest seed prices and sell values, with powerful abilities like defense, low gravity, and high repeat harvest yields.

How do I get rare seeds in Grow a Garden 2?

Most seeds are bought from the Seed Shop when they appear in rotation. Some seeds like Green Bean can be obtained via code TEAMGREENBEAN, and Ghost Pepper Pack seeds come from opening the Ghost Pepper Pack. Higher rarity seeds appear less frequently but offer much higher profits.

What does the Mushroom plant do?

Mushroom is an Epic plant that, when grown, can be jumped on to bounce you into the air. It has a high sell value and is one of the best early-game epic seeds to invest in.

Which plants have the highest sell value?

Plants like Dragon's Breath, Moon Bloom, Venus Fly Trap, Pomegranate, and Poison Apple have the highest base sell values. Their seed prices are also very high, but they yield the best returns when harvested with mutations and boosts.

What are mutations in Grow a Garden 2?

Mutations are special variations of plants that can appear during growth. They include Gold, Rainbow, Electric, Frozen, Starstruck, and Bloodlit. Mutations significantly increase the sell value of the crop and are highly sought after for profit farming.
