Understanding Plant Values

Exponential Weight

Grow a Garden 2 uses an exponential formula. As weight increases, the price jump is much larger. High-tier plants like Mushroom scale massively with even 1kg of extra weight.

Decay Mechanics

Decay is the biggest enemy of profit. A plant left unwatered or unharvested for too long loses its "Fresh" status, dropping its value by up to 80%.

Stacking Boosts

Maximize sheckles by combining Mutations and Friend Boosts. A 70% friend boost on a Bloodlit mutation is the meta for top-tier players.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to calculate plant value?

Select the plant, mutation, and enter the weight. Our calculator uses game-confirmed exponential formulas to give you the most accurate range.

Why is my plant value low?

If your plant is "Decayed", it loses 45-80% value. Always water before picking to restore the crop to "Fresh" status.

What are Band 2 & 3 rolls?

Bands are rare weight tiers. Band 1 is common, while Band 3 is ultra-rare, giving you a massive starting weight without doubles.

What is the luck cap?

Size luck from Sprinklers is capped at 100. Adding more sprinklers beyond this will not increase your doubling chance.

Best plant for sheckles?

High-tier plants like Mushroom, Dragon's Breath, and Moon Bloom are the best earners due to their high base value and exponents.

Does friend boost stack?

Yes, standing near players gives you up to 70% extra value. This boost is added after mutation multipliers.
