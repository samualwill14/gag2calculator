## Site Overview

**Grow a Garden 2 Calculator** (also known as **GAG 2 Calculator** or **GAG2 Calculator**) is the ultimate online tool suite for **Grow a Garden 2**, the popular Roblox farming simulation game. Our platform provides accurate calculators, detailed guides, and up-to-date game data to help players maximise their gardening efficiency, optimise crop values, plan pet growth, and master every aspect of the game.

## 🌿 Plant Value Calculator (Homepage)

**URL:** https://gag2calc.com

Our flagship tool helps you calculate the exact value of your plants with mutations and modifiers. Features include:

- **30+ Plants Database** – Complete catalog of all Grow a Garden 2 crops
- **50+ Mutations System** – Including Rainbow, Golden, Shocked, Frozen, and rare admin mutations
- **Advanced Multipliers** – Calculate complex mutation combinations accurately
- **Weight Optimisation** – Fine-tune plant weight for maximum value
- **Batch Calculator** – Calculate value for up to 200 plants at once
- **Friend Boost Settings** – Factor in 0–50% friend bonuses
- **Value‑to‑Weight Converter** – Reverse calculation to find required plant weight

**Additional Tools:**
- [Plant Growth Calculator](https://gag2calc.com/plant-growth/) – Track and predict plant growth times
- [Weight Chance Calculator](https://gag2calc.com/weight-chance/) – Calculate probability of specific plant weights
